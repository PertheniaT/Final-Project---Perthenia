About

Hi and welcome to Peachy Travel Tips. I’m Perthenia originally from Cape Town, South Africa. Ever since I was a little kid traveling the world was on top of my list, so in 2016 I packed my bags and moved to London. London is a great base for travellers and I haven’t looked back since. People always ask me about the trips I’ve been on, how I planned it, how much was it, what tours did I do etc - so I thought I’d put together a guide of my personal experience and recommendations. I’m not a professional but I do love sharing advice and tips on where to go and what to do, and of course I’m a firm believer that travel is good for the soul. Lets go, adverture awaits!
