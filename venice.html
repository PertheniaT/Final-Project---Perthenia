4 fun facts

Venice got it's first female gondolier in 2010

One of the narrowest streets in the world is located in Venice. It is only 53cm wide and called Calletta

Venice has 417 bridges. 72 of these bridges are private. The most famous bridges are the Rialto Bridge and the Bridge Of Sighs

Venetian folklore says that if a couple in a gondola kiss as they pass underneath each bridge they will remain in love forever.

Speak like a local

Buongiorno
Used as a well-wishing greeting but usually only used in the morning until noon

Dai
Can be used for exclamations like “come on!” or disbelief, like “really?!”.

Prego
Can have different meanings like “You’re welcome”, “After you”, “Please come in”, “Please take a seat”

Chin chin (cin cin)
Commonly used when cheering to an ocasion
